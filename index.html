What is crowdfunding?

  1. raising money from the mass market for a project or business
  2. three types of actors
    1. project initiator
    2. individuals
    3. moderation organization

History of crowdfunding

  1. 1885: campaign for Pedestal of the Statue of Liberty
  2. 1997: Marillion funds US tour
  3. 2005: Kiva (micro-lending platform)
  4. 2000: ArtistShare: first crowdfunding platform
  5. 2006: First record of the word “crowdfunding”

Types of

crowdfunding

  1. Reward-based
    1. investors receive a tangible item or service
    2. special rewards
  1. Donation-based
    1. receiving nothing in return

  1. Equity-based
    1. receive a stake/shares in the company
    2. provision of finance in the form of equity
    3. most commonly been used to fund startups

    1. investors get the money back
